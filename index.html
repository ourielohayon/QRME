About QRME

Welcome to QRME, a new app created to make it easy and fast to connect in real life (IRL).

Just list all your links and profile details, QRME will automatically create your personal page with all the QR codes ready to be scanned when you meet someone. It also generates a smart greeting message ready to copy with your location, date and event name.
